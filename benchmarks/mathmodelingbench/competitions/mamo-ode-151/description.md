# Mamo Ode 151 A Freshly Baked Loaf Of Bread Is Removed From The Oven At A Tempera

A freshly baked loaf of bread is removed from the oven at a temperature of 109°C and placed in a room with an ambient temperature of 42°C. The rate constant \(k\) for the cooling process, according to Newton's Law of Cooling, is found to be \(0.06 \, \text{min}^{-1}\). Calculate the temperature of the loaf after 7 minutes. Provide your answer in °C and up to three decimal places.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
