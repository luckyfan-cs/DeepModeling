# Mamo Ode 26 A Large Aquarium Initially Contains 5581 Liters Of Water With A Salt

A large aquarium initially contains \(5581\) liters of water with a salt concentration of \(69\) grams per liter. After 25 minutes, how many kilograms of salt will be in the aquarium? Please provide your answer to five significant figures.
