# Mamo Ode 142 A Freshly Baked Loaf Of Bread Is Removed From The Oven At A Tempera

A freshly baked loaf of bread is removed from the oven at a temperature of 152°C and placed in a room with an ambient temperature of 18°C. After 17 minutes, the temperature of the bread has decreased to approximately 105.605°C. Using Newton's Law of Cooling, what would be the expected temperature of the bread after 18 minutes? Provide your answer in °C and round it to six significant figures.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.
