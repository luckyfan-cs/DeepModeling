# Bwor 70 A Machine Tool Has 5 Hours Of Available Working Time Per Day

A machine tool has 5 hours of available working time per day. Producing one unit of Product A or Product B each requires 1 hour. The costs per unit are 4 yuan for A and 3 yuan for B. The selling prices of each product depend linearly on their respective output quantities, as follows: 

  $$
  \begin{array}{l}{p_{1}\,=\,12-x_{1}}\\ {p_{2}\,=\,13-2x_{2}}\end{array}
  $$  

 where $x_{1} and x_{2}$ represent the quantities produced of Product A and Product B, respectively. Question: If the machine must operate 5 hours per day, how many units of A and B should be produced to maximize total profit?
