# Mamo Ode 143 A Blacksmith Heats An Iron Rod To A Temperature Of 72 C And Then Le

A blacksmith heats an iron rod to a temperature of 72°C and then leaves it to cool in a room where the ambient temperature is constantly maintained at 10°C. After 4 minutes, the temperature of the rod has decreased to 39.112°C. Using Newton's Law of Cooling, calculate the temperature of the iron rod after 15 minutes. Provide your answer in °C and up to five significant figures.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
