# Mamo Ode 138 A Freshly Baked Loaf Of Bread At A Temperature Of 73 C Is Placed In

A freshly baked loaf of bread at a temperature of 73°C is placed in a room where the ambient temperature is constant at 27°C. After 5 minutes, the temperature of the bread has decreased to approximately 62.83°C. Using Newton's Law of Cooling, calculate the estimated temperature of the bread after 11 minutes. Provide your answer in °C and to two decimal places.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
