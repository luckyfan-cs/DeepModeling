# Mamo Ode 104 In A Large City With 9 322 Residents A Rumor Starts Spreading

In a large city with 9,322 residents, a rumor starts spreading. At the initial time t = 0 days, 337 people have already heard the rumor. The rate of spread of the rumor is 0.733 per day. According to a model where the rate of spread is proportional to the product of the numbers of the population who have heard the rumor and the numbers who have not, can you determine how many people will have heard about this rumor after 10 days? Provide your answer in whole numbers.
