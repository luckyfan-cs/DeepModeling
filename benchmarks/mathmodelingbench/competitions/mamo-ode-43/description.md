# Mamo Ode 43 A Remote Island Has A Population Of 10 000 People

A remote island has a population of 10,000 people. The population grows naturally at a rate of 2% per year. In addition to this natural growth, the number of people coming to the island as immigrants can be modeled by the function f(t) = e^t, where t is the number of years since the current year. What is the population of the island after 2 year, rounded to the nearest whole number? Keep 5 significant figures.
