# Mamo Ode 116 In A Remote Village With 3258 Inhabitants A Rumor Starts Spreading

In a remote village with 3258 inhabitants, a rumor starts spreading. At the initial time t = 0 days, 792 people have already heard the rumor. The rate of spread of the rumor is 0.531 per day. According to a model where the rate of spread is proportional to the product of the numbers of the population who have heard the rumor and the numbers who have not, can you determine how many people will have heard this rumor after 8 days? Provide your answer in whole numbers.
