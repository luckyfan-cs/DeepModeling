# Mamo Ode 148 A Freshly Baked Loaf Of Bread At A Temperature Of 200 C Is Left To 

A freshly baked loaf of bread at a temperature of 200°C is left to cool in a room with a constant ambient temperature of 19°C. The rate constant \(k\) for the cooling process, according to Newton's Law of Cooling, is found to be \(0.034 \, \text{min}^{-1}\). Calculate the temperature of the loaf after 8 minutes. Provide your answer in °C and rounded up to three decimal places.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
