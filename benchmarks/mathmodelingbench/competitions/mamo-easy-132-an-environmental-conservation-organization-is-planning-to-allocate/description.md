# Mamo Easy 132 An Environmental Conservation Organization Is Planning To Allocate

An environmental conservation organization is planning to allocate resources between two projects: Project X and Project Y. These projects could involve restoring habitats, promoting sustainable practices, conducting research, or raising awareness about environmental issues. Provide your answer rounded to the nearest whole number.
