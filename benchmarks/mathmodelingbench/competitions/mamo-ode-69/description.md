# Mamo Ode 69 A Company Has An Initial Capital Of 48008

A company has an initial capital of $48008. It makes a continuous profit at a rate proportional to the current amount of money, with a proportionality constant of 0.427 per year. Additionally, it also earns a fixed income of $1441 per year. How much will the company have after 5 years? Provide your answer in dollars and to nine significant figures.
