# Mamo Ode 227 In An Electrical Circuit Consisting Of A Resistor An Inductor And A

Calculate how much charge will be on the capacitor at \(t = 8\) seconds after closing the circuit? Assume that increasing charge is considered positive. Provide your answer in coulombs rounded to three significant figures.
