# Mamo Ode 139 A Frozen Chicken At A Temperature Of 4 C Is Placed In An Oven To Th

A frozen chicken at a temperature of -4°C is placed in an oven to thaw. The oven has a constant ambient temperature of 37°C. After 13 minutes, the temperature of the chicken has increased to 28.714°C. Using Newton's Law of Heating, calculate the temperature of the chicken after 17 minutes. Provide your answer in °C and up to three decimal places.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
