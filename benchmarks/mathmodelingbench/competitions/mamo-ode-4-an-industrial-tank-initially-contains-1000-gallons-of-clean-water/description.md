# Mamo Ode 4 An Industrial Tank Initially Contains 1000 Gallons Of Clean Water

Given the dynamic process of waste water inflow and mixture outflow, calculate the concentration of waste in the tank after 100 minutes. Use the provided model to express the waste concentration as a fraction of the inflow concentration. Keep you answer to 3 significant figures.
