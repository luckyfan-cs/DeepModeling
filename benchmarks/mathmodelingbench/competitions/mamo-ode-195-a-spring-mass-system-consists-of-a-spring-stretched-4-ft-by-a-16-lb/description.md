# Mamo Ode 195 A Spring Mass System Consists Of A Spring Stretched 4 Ft By A 16 Lb

Considering downward from the equilibrium position as positive, calculate the mass's position at \(t = 2\) seconds. Provide the position in feet, rounded to four significant figures.
