# Mamo Ode 2 A Patient Is Given A Continuous Infusion Of A Drug At A Constant Rate

A patient is given a continuous infusion of a drug at a constant rate of 20 mg/hr. The drug is removed from the bloodstream at a rate proportional to the current concentration, with a proportionality constant of 0.5 hr^-1. If the initial concentration of the drug at time 0 is 0 mg, what will be the concentration of the drug in the patient's bloodstream after a long time? Keep 2 significant figures.
