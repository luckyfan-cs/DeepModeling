# Mamo Ode 137 A Cup Of Tea Is Initially At A Temperature Of 5 C And Is Left To Co

A cup of tea is initially at a temperature of 5°C and is left to cool in a room with a constant ambient temperature of 2°C. After 3 hours, the temperature of the tea has decreased to 4.929°C. Using Newton's Law of Cooling, calculate the temperature of the tea after 5 hours. Provide your answer in °C and keep three decimal places.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
