# Mamo Ode 115 In A Small City With 9507 Inhabitants A Rumor Starts Spreading

In a small city with 9507 inhabitants, a rumor starts spreading. At the initial time t = 0 days, 2427 people have already heard the rumor. The rate of spread of the rumor is 0.666 per day. According to a model where the rate of spread is proportional to the product of the numbers of the population who have heard the rumor and the numbers who have not, can you determine how many people will have heard the rumor after 8 days? Provide your answer in nearest whole number.
