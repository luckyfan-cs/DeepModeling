The goal is to minimize the total cost while ensuring maximum effectiveness.\n\nGiven these conditions and considering that the investments need to be whole numbers due to practical reasons such as contract terms or payment methods:\nWhat is the minimum total cost required for this marketing campaign? Provide your answer rounded to the nearest dollar.
