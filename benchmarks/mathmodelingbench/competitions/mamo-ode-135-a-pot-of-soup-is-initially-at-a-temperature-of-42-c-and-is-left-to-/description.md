# Mamo Ode 135 A Pot Of Soup Is Initially At A Temperature Of 42 C And Is Left To 

A pot of soup is initially at a temperature of 42°C and is left to cool in a room with a constant ambient temperature of 7°C. After 11 minutes, the temperature of the soup has decreased to approximately 31.079°C. By applying Newton's Law of Cooling, calculate the temperature of the soup after 20 minutes. Provide your answer in °C and in five significant figures.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
