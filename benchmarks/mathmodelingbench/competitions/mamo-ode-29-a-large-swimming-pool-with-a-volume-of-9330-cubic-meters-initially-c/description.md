# Mamo Ode 29 A Large Swimming Pool With A Volume Of 9330 Cubic Meters Initially C

The well-mixed water is drained out at the same rate to keep the volume constant. What will be the amount of chlorine in kilograms remaining in the pool after \(30\) minutes? Express your answer to four significant figures.
