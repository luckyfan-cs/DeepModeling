Given that the cost per unit of effectiveness for channel X is \$5, for channel Y is \$4, and for channel Z is \$3, while trying to minimize the total cost(X,Y,Z are integers). Find out what would be the minimum possible total cost in dollars? Provide your answer rounded to the nearest dollar.
