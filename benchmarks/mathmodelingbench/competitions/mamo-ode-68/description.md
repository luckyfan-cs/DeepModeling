# Mamo Ode 68 A Small Business Starts With An Initial Capital Of 63 868

A small business starts with an initial capital of $63,868. The company's profits grow continuously at a rate of 47.2% per year and the company also makes continuous deposits into their account at a rate of $4,901 per year. How much total capital will the company have after 8 years? Provide your answer in dollars and to ten significant figures.
