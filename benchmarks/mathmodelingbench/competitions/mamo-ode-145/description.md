# Mamo Ode 145 A Car Has Been Parked Outside During A Winter Night And Its Engine 

A car has been parked outside during a winter night and its engine is at -2°C in the morning. The ambient temperature is 25°C. After starting, it takes 15 minutes for the engine to warm up to 12.807°C. According to Newton's Law of Cooling (which also applies to heating), what will be the temperature of the car engine after 20 minutes? Provide your answer in °C and in five significant figures.

### Submission Format
- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@value[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.

### Evaluation
- Submissions are evaluated using an exact-match checker with a small tolerance (±1e-2) on the reported value.
