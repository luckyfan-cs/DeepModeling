# Mamo Easy 6 A Marketing Manager Is Planning A New Campaign And Has Two Channels 

A marketing manager is planning a new campaign and has two channels to choose from, $X$ and $Y$. Both channels have different costs per unit of effectiveness: $\$5$ for channel $X$ and $\$3$ for channel $Y$. The total effectiveness score, calculated as the sum of the units allocated to each channel, must be at least 1000 points. Furthermore, the difference in units between channel X and twice that of channel Y should not exceed 500 units to maintain a balanced strategy. Given these constraints and aiming to minimize the total cost (assuming both X and Y are integers due to budget limitations), what would be the minimum cost needed for this campaign? Provide your answer rounded to the nearest dollar.

- Provide a single row with `id` and `answer` columns.
- The answer must use the template `@cost[value]`, where `value` is the numeric solution rounded as appropriate.
